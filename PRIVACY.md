# Privacy Policy — InlineDoubt

InlineDoubt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- All doubts are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local
- No data is sent to any third-party server
- No tracking or analytics of any kind
- Session cookies are only used to call the AI platform you are already logged into
