# Privacy Policy — InlineDoubt

Last updated: July 2026

## Overview
InlineDoubt is a Chrome Extension that allows users 
to ask inline doubts about specific lines in AI chat 
answers. This privacy policy explains how we handle 
your data.

## Data We Collect
InlineDoubt does NOT collect any personal data.

Specifically, we do not collect:
- Names, email addresses, or any identifying information
- Browsing history or web activity
- Chat content or AI conversation history
- Location data
- Payment or financial information

## Data Storage
The only data stored by InlineDoubt is:
- Your inline doubts and AI replies
- This data is stored LOCALLY in your browser only
  using chrome.storage.local
- This data NEVER leaves your device
- This data is NOT sent to any server we operate

## Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties.
We do not sell any data.
We do not transfer any data outside your browser.

## Permissions Used
- storage: To save your doubts locally in your browser
- cookies: To read your existing AI platform session
  cookie so the extension can call the AI on your 
  behalf — the cookie is never stored or transmitted
  to any third party server

## Session Cookies
InlineDoubt reads your existing session cookie from 
Claude.ai or ChatGPT only to make API calls to those 
platforms on your behalf — exactly as if you typed 
the question yourself. The cookie value is never 
logged, stored, or sent anywhere else.

## Changes to This Policy
If we update this policy, we will update the 
"Last updated" date above.
